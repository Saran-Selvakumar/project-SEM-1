MAD ABOUT YOU






Though the cover might convey something different,
Mad About You is more of a women’s fiction than a romance. In Harriet, we see a woman who has undergone
a lot of emotional abuse and gaslighting. We see a woman who despite her
hardships, finally finds the courage to stand up
for herself. And this somewhat redeems her too. The way her
character grows and develops, navigating life issues
and battling everyday challenges, is surely a treat to read.
